안녕하세요!

I'm Jongwon, a bioengineering researcher developing point-of-care molecular diagnostic platforms for infectious disease detection, with a focus on blood drying techniques and CRISPR-based assays.

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Sabeti Lab at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard. Prior to joining the Broad, I was a Professor Joe Greene Postdoctoral Fellow in the Bashir Lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

My research focuses on diagnostics for respiratory viruses (SARS-CoV-2, Influenza, RSV) and bloodstream infections (Sepsis, Hepatitis B). I developed a biphasic blood drying method enabling purification-free pathogen detection, and an amplification-free OR-gated CRISPR-Cascade assay achieving attomolar sensitivity.

Outside the lab, I enjoy spending time with my family — my wife and our son.
