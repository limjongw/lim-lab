I'm Jongwon, a bioengineering researcher developing point-of-care molecular diagnostic platforms for infectious disease detection, with a focus on blood drying techniques and CRISPR-based assays.

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Sabeti Lab at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard. Previously, I was a Professor Joe Greene Postdoctoral Fellow in the Bashir Lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

My vision is to establish a new field of blood engineering for diagnostics — integrating research, teaching, mentoring, and outreach to become scientifically influential and foster next-generation scientific leaders.

Amplification-free CRISPR-Cascade for blood pathogen detection

2022 – 2026 · Bashir Lab, UIUC
CRISPROR-gate AttomolarWhole bloodAmplification-free

Developed an amplification-free, OR-gated CRISPR-Cascade assay with dual-blocking autocatalytic strategy achieving attomolar sensitivity for multiplex pathogen detection directly from whole blood — eliminating nucleic acid purification.

PNAS (2025) PNAS (2026)

Biphasic dried blood diagnostics

2020 – 2026 · Bashir Lab, UIUC
Blood dryingHBV SepsisLAMPRPAZikaHCV

Developed a biphasic (solid + liquid phase) blood drying method enabling inhibitor inactivation and DNA/RNA amplification simultaneously without purification. Established a new material platform — dried blood — for molecular diagnostics. Achieved 10 IU mL⁻¹ LOD for HBV within 1.5 hours.

Advanced Healthcare Materials (2024) Small (2024) PNAS (2022) Science Advances (2026, accepted)

Multiplex point-of-care respiratory virus detection

2020 – 2024 · Bashir Lab, UIUC
SARS-CoV-2Influenza RSVMicrofluidicsSmartphone

Built a smartphone-integrated point-of-care platform with temperature control, fluorescence reading, and contamination-free cartridges for simultaneous detection of SARS-CoV-2, Influenza, and RSV, including variant identification.

ACS Sensors (2024) Lab on a Chip (2022) ACS Sensors (2021)

Rapid, culture-free bloodstream infection diagnostics

2024 – 2026 · Bashir Lab, UIUC & Sabeti Lab, Broad Institute
SepsisCulture-free Gram typingLAMP

Developed a culture-free biphasic duplex-LAMP assay for gram-typing bacteria panels directly from whole blood, enabling rapid sepsis diagnosis without lengthy culture steps.
